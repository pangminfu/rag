# Acme Robotics — Leave Policy

This document describes the leave entitlements for all full-time employees of Acme Robotics. It applies globally unless local labor law mandates a more generous benefit, in which case the local statutory rule takes precedence.

## Annual Leave

New full-time employees are granted **18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year**, accrued at a rate of 1.5 days per completed month of service. After three years of continuous employment, the entitlement increases to **22 days**, and after seven years it increases to **25 days**.

Annual leave must be requested at least **two weeks in advance** through the HR portal (`https://hr.acme-robotics.internal`). Requests are reviewed by the employee's direct manager within three business days. Up to **5 unused days** may be carried over to the next calendar year, but they must be used by **31 March** of the following year or they will be forfeited.

Employees may not take more than **10 consecutive working days** of annual leave without written approval from a department director.

## Sick Leave

Each employee is entitled to **10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year**. Sick leave does not accrue and unused days are not carried over. A medical certificate from a registered physician is required for any continuous absence of **three or more days**. Sick leave used for elective procedures requires pre-approval from HR.

If an employee exhausts their paid sick leave, additional time off may be deducted from annual leave with the manager's consent, or taken as unpaid leave.

## Parental Leave

- **Maternity leave:** 16 weeks fully paid. The leave can begin up to four weeks before the expected due date and must be taken in a single continuous block.
- **Paternity leave / secondary caregiver leave:** 6 weeks fully paid, to be taken within the first six months following the birth or adoption of a child.
- **Adoption leave:** 12 weeks fully paid for the primary caregiver, regardless of the child's age, provided adoption is legally finalized.

Parental leave does not affect annual leave accrual. Employees returning from parental leave are guaranteed return to the same role and salary band.

## Bereavement Leave

Up to **5 days** of paid bereavement leave is provided for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling, grandparent, or in-law). Up to **2 days** is provided for extended family members.

## Unpaid Leave

Unpaid leave of up to 90 days may be requested for personal reasons (such as extended travel, education, or family care) and requires approval from both the direct manager and HR. Benefits such as health insurance continue during approved unpaid leave, but the employee is responsible for any premium contributions.

## How to Apply

All leave types are requested through the HR portal. Emergency sick leave may be reported by email to `hr-leave@acme-robotics.internal` and formalized in the portal upon return.
