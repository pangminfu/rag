# Acme Robotics — Customer FAQ

This FAQ collects the most common questions our customers ask about Acme Robotics products, ordering, support, and security.

## Ordering and Delivery

**Q: How do I place an order?**
You can place an order through your Acme account portal at `https://shop.acme-robotics.internal`, by emailing `sales@acme-robotics.internal`, or by contacting your assigned account manager. For orders over USD 50,000, a signed master service agreement is required.

**Q: What is the typical lead time?**
Standard configurations of the R-200 ship within **5 business days** from one of our regional warehouses (US, EU, APAC). The X-9 Inspection Drone ships within **10 business days** because each unit is calibrated and bench-tested before dispatch. Custom configurations may take 4–8 weeks.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
Yes. Acme Robotics ships to over 40 countries. Customers are responsible for any local import duties, taxes, and regulatory approvals. Some countries restrict imports of LiPo batteries; contact sales for guidance.

## Returns and Refunds

**Q: Can I return a product I'm not satisfied with?**
Unopened, unused products may be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund minus return shipping. Products that have been activated, registered, or used are subject to a 15% restocking fee and must pass an Acme inspection before a refund is issued.

**Q: What if my unit arrives damaged?**
Notify `support@acme-robotics.internal` within **72 hours** of delivery, including photos of the packaging and the unit. Damaged-in-transit units are replaced at no charge.

## Warranty and Support

**Q: How long is the warranty?**
The **R-200** is covered for **2 years**, and the **X-9** is covered for **1 year** from the date of purchase. Warranty terms are described in detail in each product's specification document.

**Q: What does the warranty cover?**
Manufacturing defects and component failures under normal use. It does not cover wear-and-tear consumables, damage from misuse, or unauthorized modifications.

**Q: How do I get support?**
Email `support@acme-robotics.internal` or open a ticket via the Acme support portal. Our standard support hours are 09:00–18:00 in your local region, Monday through Friday. Customers on the AcmeCare Plus or AcmeCare Air plans have access to 24/7 support.

## Security and Data

**Q: Where is my fleet data stored?**
By default, fleet telemetry is stored in the Acme cloud region closest to your registered company address (US-East, EU-Frankfurt, or AP-Singapore). Customers may opt for an on-premises Fleet Console deployment.

**Q: Is the data encrypted?**
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Each device authenticates using a unique X.509 certificate provisioned during manufacturing.

## Training

**Q: Do you offer training?**
Yes. We offer one-day operator training for the X-9 and a half-day administrator training for the Fleet Console. Online self-paced versions are also available.

## Pricing

**Q: Where can I see pricing?**
Pricing is published in your account portal once a sales agreement is in place. Volume and multi-year discounts are available — contact your account manager for a quote.
